Our History

Founded in 1954. Built on Innovation.

Thermo Tech Inc. has operated reliably and continuously since 1954. Our company started as a manufacturer's rep organization providing sales and service of hydronic heating and industrial equipment. Soon, we were involved in fabricating stainless steel flexible hoses and connectors for aerospace.

With our innovative process of bonding a proprietary, food grade silicone lining onto stainless steel, we produce sanitary, food grade flexible connectors and transfer hoses for the food processing, dairy, pharmaceutical and beverage industries across the country.

Our Journey

Seven Decades of Innovation

From a manufacturer's rep organization to an industry-leading custom manufacturer — our story of continuous evolution.

1954

Company Founded

Thermo Tech Inc. is established in Denver, Colorado as a manufacturer's representative organization providing sales and service of hydronic heating and industrial equipment.

1960s

Aerospace Expansion

The company begins fabricating stainless steel flexible hoses and connectors for the aerospace industry, developing precision manufacturing expertise that would define its future.

1970s

Industrial Piping Solutions

Thermo Tech adapts its aerospace-grade piping products to provide practical solutions for vibration isolation, thermal expansion, and misalignment in HVAC and industrial piping systems.

1980s

Silicone Bonding Breakthrough

Development of the proprietary process for bonding food-grade silicone onto stainless steel — a breakthrough that opens the door to the sanitary food processing market.

1990s

Sanitary Market Leadership

Thermo Tech becomes a leading supplier of sanitary flexible connectors and transfer hoses for the food processing, dairy, pharmaceutical, and beverage industries nationwide.

2000s

Facilities Expansion

The company relocates to the expanded facility at 12401 E 37th Ave in Denver, adding advanced machining, welding, and testing capabilities to meet growing demand.

Today

Custom Manufacturing Excellence

Serving 20+ industries across North America with a full range of custom-manufactured flexible connectors, expansion joints, and compensators in rubber, metal, and PTFE — all built in Denver.

Industries We Serve

Industries & Markets We Serve

From food plants to power stations, our piping solutions are hard at work across North America.

Food & Beverage Processing

Thermo Tech manufactures FDA and 3A compliant sanitary flexible connectors, silicone-lined transfer hoses, and expansion joints for food production plants, dairies, breweries, distilleries, wineries, and beverage bottling facilities. Our smooth bore interior designs are CIP-compatible, ensuring hygienic product conveyance and easy cleaning between production runs.

HVAC & Mechanical Systems

Our industrial flexible connectors, rubber expansion joints, and expansion compensators isolate vibration, absorb thermal movement, and reduce noise in heating, ventilation, air conditioning, plumbing, and refrigeration piping systems. Available with flanged, grooved, threaded, and copper sweat end connections in pipe sizes from 1/2" to 12" and beyond.

Pharmaceutical & Biotech

We produce high-purity sanitary connectors and PTFE-lined hose assemblies for pharmaceutical manufacturing, biotech labs, and cleanroom environments. Our products resist aggressive cleaning chemicals and repeated steam sterilization cycles, meeting the stringent hygiene standards required in regulated pharmaceutical production.

Oil, Gas & Power Generation

Thermo Tech's heavy-duty stainless steel bellows expansion joints, flexible metal connectors, and braided metal hose assemblies handle the extreme pressures, temperatures, and vibration found in oil and gas pipelines, power plants, steam systems, and natural gas distribution networks — tested up to 3,000 PSI.

Water & Waste Water Treatment

Our rubber connectors, expansion joints, and flexible metal hoses serve water filtration plants and waste water treatment facilities across the country. These products compensate for pipe misalignment, absorb pump vibration, and handle the thermal cycling common in water treatment infrastructure.

A flexible connector is a piping component designed to absorb movement, vibration, noise, and misalignment in piping systems. You need one whenever your piping system experiences thermal expansion/contraction, mechanical vibration from pumps or compressors, seismic movement, or misalignment between pipe runs.
While both absorb movement, expansion joints are primarily designed to handle axial (lengthwise) thermal expansion and contraction using bellows elements. Flexible connectors are designed to absorb vibration, noise, and multi-directional movement. We manufacture both types in a variety of materials and configurations.
